Chocolate Chip Cookies

Not typically soft and doughy—but instead thin, crispy deliciously chewy. And extremely difficult to eat just one.

Yield
18-20 cookies
Prep time
20 minutes
Cook time
11 minutes

Nutritional information

Serving size: 1 cookie

Calories
68
Fat
3.5 g
Sodium
42 mg
Carbohydrates

Protein
1 g

Ingredients

  • 7 tbsp soy-free margarine
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • ¼ cup cane sugar
  • 1 flax egg: 1 tbsp ground flax mixed with 3 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup oat flour
  • 1 cup almond Flour
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ cup dark chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350° and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix together the flax egg and set aside.
  3. With an electric mixer, beat the margarine and oil until fluffy.
  4. Add the sugars and beat for another 1–2 minutes until creamy.
  5. Beat in the flax egg & remaining ingredients.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Shape balls of dough and place them on the baking sheet.
  8. Bake for about 10-11 minutes, until slightly golden around the edges.
  9. Allow them to cool for 5–10 minutes on the baking sheet and then transfer to a cooling rack for another 10 minutes.
